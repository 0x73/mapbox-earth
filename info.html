MapBox Earth is a fun app for exploring and comparing MapBox maps in 3D. Just choose a map from the selection bar, then spin and pan around as much as you like. Enjoy!

Brought to you by MapBox with the open source WhirlyGlobe-Maply toolkit from mousebird consulting.

This app itself is also open source. See the launch blog post for more info.
